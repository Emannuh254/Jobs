Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the homepage. Fill in your details including your name, email address, and password. Once submitted, you'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

How do I search for jobs?

You can search for jobs by using the search bar on the Jobs page. Enter keywords related to the job title, company, or skills you're looking for. You can also filter results by job type, location, and other criteria using the filter chips below the search bar.

How do I apply for a job?

To apply for a job, first make sure you're logged in to your account. Then, click on the job you're interested in to view the full details. Click the "Apply Now" button and follow the instructions to submit your application. You may need to upload your resume and provide additional information.

How do the referrals work?

Our referral program allows you to refer candidates for job openings and earn rewards if they're hired. To refer someone, go to the Referrals page and fill out the referral form with the candidate's details. You can track the status of your referrals and see your earnings in the same section.

How do I update my profile?

To update your profile, go to the Profile page and click the "Edit Profile" button. You can update your personal information, work experience, education, skills, and other details. Keeping your profile up to date increases your chances of being noticed by employers.
